about me

For better or worse, I am a completely self-taught programmer...
I am currently a High School band teacher, and while I do love that job, I have always been fascinated by technology, so not too long ago I began teaching myself how to code.

I didn't initially have any specific goals in mind, and I certainly wasn't looking for a career change, but as I started to dig in I discovered that I actually really liked it. I found myself spending almost all my free time doing projects, reading articles and going through The Odin Project, at an accelerated rate.

Only three months after beginning to learn, I was invited to join the moderating team at The Odin Project, where I hang around helping new learners, and contributing to the ongoing improvement of the curriculum.

As far as my personal life, I live in Missouri with my wife and three kids. Besides programming I have an intense love for music. I play guitar and compose music, which you can check out here, if you like.
